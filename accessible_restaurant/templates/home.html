Provides reviews and ratings of NYC restaurants with a specific focus on wheelchair accessibility and quality of experience for disabled patrons.

People in WheelChair

Our website mainly focus on people with disability, especially people in wheelchair.

They can check whether a restaurant is compliant or not, get accessibility accommodation information about the restaurants.

Accessible Checklists

Five checklists for more standardized evaluation.

  • Level entry or ramp
  • Accessible 32-inch wide door
  • Accessible 36-inch wide path
  • Accessible tables
  • Accessible restroom
